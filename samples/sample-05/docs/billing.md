# Billing & Plans

This page explains Acme Cloud pricing, how billing works, and our refund
policy.

## Plans

| Plan       | Price        | Best for                          |
|------------|--------------|-----------------------------------|
| Free       | $0/month     | Trying things out, solo projects  |
| Pro        | $20/month    | Small teams up to 10 people       |
| Business   | $99/month    | Growing teams with advanced needs |
| Enterprise | Custom       | Large orgs needing SSO & SLAs     |

All paid plans are billed monthly or yearly. Yearly billing saves you two
months (roughly 17%).

## How billing works

Your subscription renews automatically on the same day each month. You can
view invoices and update your card under **Settings → Billing**. If a payment
fails, we'll retry for 7 days before pausing your workspace.

## Refunds

We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** on all new paid subscriptions. If
you're not happy within the first 14 days, we'll give you a full refund — no
questions asked.

To request a refund, email **billing@acme.cloud** from the address on your
account, or open **Settings → Billing → Request a refund**. Refunds are
processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Note: refunds apply to the most recent charge only. Renewals after the first
14 days are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges.

## Cancelling

To cancel, go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel plan**. You'll keep access
until the end of your current billing period, then drop to the Free plan.
