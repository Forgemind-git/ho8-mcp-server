# Billing & Plans

## Available plans

Acme SaaS offers three plans: Starter, Pro, and Enterprise.

### Starter plan ($12/seat/month)
- Up to 5 seats
- 10 GB storage
- Email support

### Pro plan ($29/seat/month)
- Up to 50 seats
- 100 GB storage
- Priority email + chat support
- API access

### Enterprise plan ($79/seat/month)
- Unlimited seats
- Unlimited storage
- Dedicated support manager
- Custom SSO / SAML
- SLA guarantee

## Annual billing

Switch to annual billing and save up to 25%.
Pro annual: 20% discount. Enterprise annual: 25% discount.
Starter plan does not offer an annual discount.

## Invoices

Invoices are emailed automatically on the billing date.
Download past invoices at Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

## Cancellation

You can cancel at any time from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan.
Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
No refunds are issued for partial periods.
