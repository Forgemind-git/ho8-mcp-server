Acme Cloud — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: On the sign-in page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email. We'll
   send you a reset link that's valid for 1 hour. Open it and choose a new
   password.

Q: Which browsers are supported?
A: Acme Cloud works on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and
   Edge. We recommend keeping your browser up to date for the best experience.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes. Go to Settings -> Data Sources -> Export. You can download your data
   as CSV or JSON at any time. Exports include all projects in your workspace.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@acme.cloud or use the in-app chat bubble in the bottom-right
   corner. Pro and Business plans get priority responses within one business
   day.

Q: Is my data backed up?
A: Yes. We back up all workspaces daily and keep backups for 30 days. Enterprise
   plans can request longer retention.
