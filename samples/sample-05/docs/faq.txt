Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my plan at any time?
A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated.
   Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
   At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan or your account is paused.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and PayPal.
   Enterprise customers can pay by wire transfer — contact sales@acme.io.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to https://app.acme.io/forgot-password and enter your email address.
   You will receive a password reset link valid for 1 hour.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Data > Export to download all your data as JSON or CSV.
   Enterprise customers can request a full database dump via support.

Q: What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
A: You will receive an email warning at 80% and 95% usage.
   At 100%, new uploads are blocked until you upgrade or delete files.

Q: Is Acme SOC 2 certified?
A: Yes. Acme SaaS is SOC 2 Type II certified. Contact sales for the full report.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Starter: support@acme.io (48h response).
   Pro: support chat in-app (4h response).
   Enterprise: dedicated Slack channel + phone (1h response).
