# Getting Started with Acme Cloud

Welcome to Acme Cloud! This short guide walks you through your first few
minutes so you can get up and running quickly.

## 1. Sign up

Go to https://app.acme.cloud and click **Sign up**. Enter your work email
and choose a password, then check your inbox for a confirmation link. Click
the link to verify your account.

## 2. Create a workspace

A workspace is where all your projects, data, and teammates live. After you
sign in for the first time, you'll be prompted to **Create a workspace**.
Give it a name (for example, your company or team name) and pick a region.

## 3. Invite your teammates

Open **Settings → Members** and click **Invite**. Type in your teammates'
email addresses and choose a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). They'll get an
email invitation to join your workspace.

## 4. Connect a data source

Head to **Settings → Data Sources** and click **Add data source**. Acme
Cloud supports CSV uploads, PostgreSQL, and Google Sheets. Follow the prompts
to authorize the connection, and your data will start syncing automatically.

That's it! You're ready to start building. If you get stuck, check the FAQ or
reach out to support@acme.cloud.
